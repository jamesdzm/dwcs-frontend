How it works

When you book with Diamond Wedding Car Services, we will confirm a date and time to visit you and ensure we match exactly the service you want, down to the color ribbons you'd like on the car(s). We will confirm all timings and check the route. We will also check with the relevant authorities that there are no remidial works planned or road maintenance that could potentially cause a delay.

In fact, once you've booked with us, you can rest assured that you won't have any transport worries leaving you free to concentrate on other critical plans for one of the best days of your life.
